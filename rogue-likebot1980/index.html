Rogue-likebot 1980 was my first bot. It was a Facebook Bot, originally a Discord Bot, that allowed users to play through a rogue-like dungeon-crawler-esque adventure.

Rogue-likebot 1980 started it's first run at the start of December 2018, and as of August 2020 the bot has almost 1400 likes.

As of May 2020, Rogue-likebot 1980 is no longer posting to Facebook, and was made open source on Github
