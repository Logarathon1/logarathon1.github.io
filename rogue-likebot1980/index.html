Rogue-likebot 1980 is a Facebook Bot, originally a Discord Bot, that allows users to play through a rogue-like dungeon-crawler-esque adventure.

Rogue-likebot 1980 started it's first run at the start of December 2018, and as of mid-August 2019 the bot has over 1250 likes.
